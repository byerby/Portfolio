Visionary Heart
Developer + Designer
Overview

I served as the web developer and assistant graphic designer, among a team of seven students. As students in the Interactive Media graduate program at Elon University, our team traveled to Matanzas, Cuba as part of the program’s “Project for the Public Good.”

Our client, Jesús Alberto Mederos, is an artist based out of the city of Matanzas, Cuba. Our team knew little of the our client’s focus before arriving in Cuba. All we knew was that he used art as a way to connect and build relationships in the communities that he serves. However, soon after meeting Mederos, someone asked a local resident how they would describe him and the work that he did. They simply responded, “Visionary Heart.” From that moment, we knew our angle in capturing the work that he does for the people.

Our core focus was to use the knowledge we gained from our experiences with Mederos and members of the community to inform the rest of the world and those interested in his work about what he is doing for the community. Thus, our goal was to create a non-traditional website that delivered engaging content regarding the three main programs Mederos created to reach the surrounding community.
