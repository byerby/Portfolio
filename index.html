Bio.

Hello,

I am a Master's student at Elon University, and I am currently seeking a degree in Interactive Media. My passion is creativity with a desire to implement creative strategies. This passion, creativity, and my experiences in life have led me to continually strive for new knowledge and to not be afraid to try new things. My perspective is to always be open to new ideas and knowledge which motivates me to challenge myself in strategic visual communication.

Beginning at a young age and through the years, I have developed a passion for various creative media which includes art, digital art, photography, graphic design, web design, film and video, and more recently, basic 3D modeling. However, my interests do not end there. I love to learn, and I love discovering unique ways to carry messages and communicate ideas, brands, and products with targeted efficiency, effectiveness, and attention getting creativity. Using a high-level integration of creativity, aesthetics, and strategic communication, the combination of my knowledge and experience culminates in being uniquely qualified to provide positive and productive support and a fresh perspective on various projects.
