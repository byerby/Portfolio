Overview

I served as a brand and web consultant for Stash House. I was introduced to this client in working with Jules & Gems Media. Stash House, a small design and furniture boutique, was looking to rebrand their business with a more clear indication of what the business offered. The owner referred the store as a “furniture, art, and accessories shop” and it was this saying that became the basis for my design.

During our initial client meeting, I took several spins around the shop to get an idea as to the owners style and get visual inspiration for the logo redesign. After working with the client, I landed on the idea that I would create a logo and provide a set of secondary logos to support each segment of the business.
